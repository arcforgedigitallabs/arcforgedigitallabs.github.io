About Arcforge Digital Labs

Our story

Arcforge Digital Labs LLC is a Florida limited liability company formed on April 14, 2026. The studio focuses on premium iOS strategy games, beginning with Reversi for iPhone and iPad — built for players who want thoughtful matchmaking, analysis, and daily reasons to come back without the compromises of ad-heavy free-to-play boards.

Mission

we build the kind of premium experiences we wish existed for the games we love.

Why Reversi

Across the category, players consistently push back on intrusive advertising and shallow monetization. Reversi is positioned as the anti-ad premium Reversi experience: online play, daily puzzles, tournaments, and polish first. For players who know what chess.com did for chess, Reversi brings that same improvement mindset to Othello with the first engine-based analysis tool in this product segment — Game Review breaks finished games down move by move so wins and losses become learning, not noise.

Team

Arcforge Digital Labs is a single-member LLC. There is no fabricated roster — design, engineering, and operations route through that structure so we can ship carefully and stand behind the product directly.
