Reversi for iPhone and iPad

Reversi is a premium iPhone and iPad strategy game for online play, Game Review, and daily puzzles.

The anti-ad premium Reversi experience for players who want one place to play, review, and improve. For players who know what chess.com did for chess, Reversi brings that same improvement loop to Othello with the first engine-based analysis tool for the category.

Live + async multiplayer Daily puzzle Free and Premium tiers
